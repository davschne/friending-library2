Welcome to Friending Library!

Allowing users to harness the power of social media to find great books to read, Friending Library facilitates borrowing and lending books within your social circle.

Friending library allows you to:

  • browse or search your friends' libraries to find books to read
  • request to borrow a book from a friend
  • track book requests, books borrowed, and books lent
